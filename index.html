In the heart of Bosnia, nestled within the lush green hills of Visoko, lies a mystery that has fascinated explorers, archaeologists, and thrill-seekers from around the globe.

Beneath the soil, ancient secrets wait to be uncovered — secrets that could rewrite the history of human civilization. The Bosnian Pyramids, as they have come to be known, stand as towering enigmas, their origins debated, their purpose elusive. But what if these structures are more than just relics of the past? What if they are the key to understanding lost civilizations and the untold potential of human ingenuity?

This captivating documentary delves into the enigmatic Bosnian Pyramid Valley, unraveling its secrets and exploring its profound connection to human consciousness and ancient megalithic history. Through a fascinating journey, the film investigates the phenomenon of the Bosnian pyramids, examining their intriguing structures and potential transformative properties. Blending science, history, and spirituality, it invites viewers to reconsider humanity's ancient past and its enduring mysteries.

Bartek and Karol have known each other since their university days, and the idea of finding themselves in Bosnia due to the Megaliths came up right after a camping trip among the Norwegian fjords. Initially, plans were slowed down by the lockdown, but if something is meant to happen, it will happen. That's when the "Wisdom of Nations" foundation came to their aid, which they stumbled upon "by accident."
